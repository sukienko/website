About

Behind the canvas

"I make things with words and with pictures — and sometimes both at once."

Hello! I'm Sukanya — a writer and artist with a sketchbook always nearby and too many books on my nightstand. I write poetry, short fiction, and reviews of the books and films that keep me up at night.

My art lives in those sketchbooks: botanical studies, imagined creatures, illustrated stories. Everything I make is slow, careful, and made with a lot of love.

This is the place for all of it.
