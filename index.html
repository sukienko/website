Hello! I'm Sukanya — I make things with words and with pictures, and sometimes both at once.

My writing wanders between poetry, fiction, film, and books. My art lives in sketchbooks somewhere between botanical illustration and things I can't quite name.

This is a place for all of it. Everything here was made slowly, with care, and a great deal of tea.
